The Opportunity

Construction is a $2 trillion industry running on spreadsheets and phone calls.

LV Construction builds 50–250 unit multifamily projects across Los Angeles. We generate $252M in construction billing over the next 3 years. We’re profitable. But we’re limited by how many projects our people can manage manually.

The Problem

90% of project management isn’t building—it’s coordination.

RFIs drafted by hand. Draw packages assembled over days. Cost data that’s a month old. Schedules updated weekly that are wrong by Tuesday. Subcontractor insurance tracked in spreadsheets. Every PM spends half their day on tasks a machine should handle.

Mission Control

An AI operating system built for construction.

Six modules. One platform. Trained on our own project data. Document intelligence that drafts RFIs in minutes. Real-time cost tracking tied to field progress. Automated draw packages, compliance monitoring, and scheduling—all from a single source of truth.

How It Works

Field data in. Intelligence out.

Superintendents capture progress with three taps and a photo. The platform does the rest: updates the schedule, flags risks, drafts documents, generates reports, verifies compliance, and shows every project’s status in real time. The CEO manages 12 projects from a dashboard, not from the driver’s seat.

The Scaling Mechanism

Add a project. Hire onsite staff. That’s it.

Mission Control automates 90% of the non-site work. Adding a new project requires only on-site staffing—a superintendent and a field engineer. The PMs, coordinators, accounting team, and office infrastructure absorb the incremental back-office load entirely through the platform. No new PM hire. No new coordinator. No additional accounting staff.

A traditional GC scales linearly—every new project requires proportional overhead. Mission Control breaks that relationship. The existing team handles twice the project volume because the platform does the coordination work that used to consume half of everyone’s day.

The Financials

$5M builds a $150M+ company.

The base business generates $7.5M in GC net income over 3 years from a committed $252M pipeline. A $5M equity raise funds the entire Mission Control buildout. The platform enables scaling to $160M+ in annual billing—driving net income from $2M to $11M+.

The Competitive Advantage

Algorithms can be copied. Data can’t.

Every project on the platform trains the AI on our specific operation—our subs, our schedules, our cost patterns, our building types. After 3 years and 30+ projects, we have a proprietary intelligence layer that no competitor can purchase. Off-the-shelf AI gives you generic. Mission Control gives you specific.

78% of contractors are testing AI. Less than 2% have deployed it. The window is now.
